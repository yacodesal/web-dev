Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipie

  • Gear

    • Stand Mixer
    • Spatula
    • Measuring Cups
    • Measuring Spoons
    • Butter Knife (For Leveling)
    • Baking Tray
    • Baking Sheet

  • Ingredients

    • 1c Butter (Room Temperature)
    • 3/4c Brown Sugar
    • 3/4c White Sugar
    • 1 Egg
    • 1/2 Splash Vanilla Extract
    • 1/2 Splash Almond Extract
    • 2 1/4c Flour
    • 1tsp Baking Powder
    • 1/2tsp Baking Soda
    • 1tsp Salt
    • 2c Chocolate Chips

  • Procedure

    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
    2. Cream the butter until smooth.
    3. Mix in the brown and white sugar.
    4. Scrape bowl, and mix in the egg and extracts.
    5. In a seperate bowl, mix together the flour,baking powder, baking soda,and salt.
    6. With the mixer on low, add the flour in 3 parts.DO NOT MIX ALL THE WAY!
    7. Before the flour in fully combined, add the chocolate chips
    8. Roll the cookie dough into balls of about 1 tbsp, and place 12 of them on the tray.
    9. Bake in the oven for 9-10 minutes, then let then cool for 15 minutes.

  • Enjoy!
